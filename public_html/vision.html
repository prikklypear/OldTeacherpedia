Welcome to Teacherpedia

Launched in September 2018, Teacherpedia is driven to make the lives of teachers easier.

Our short term vision is to create inspiring, customisable resources that make creating resources easier than ever. All our resources will be customisable. Crucially however, our resources will be regenerative - that means that our resources can be regenerated with new questions time and time again.

Our Vision

In the future, we will develop a unique system of planning lessons, using adaptive and customisable resources that can be tailored to your curriculum and subject needs.
